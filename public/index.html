The physics behind it

How to read this. Scores are a comparative model (0–100) built from string material, geometry, gauge, tension and your racket's pattern & stiffness — blended for hybrids with the mains weighted more heavily, since they carry most of the ball. They describe tendencies, not guarantees: your technique, swing speed, arm history and the specific ball matter as much as the string. "What it's known for" and manufacturer claims are summarized from how each product is marketed and commonly reviewed. Treat this as a smart starting point for your next string job, not a medical or absolute verdict — especially arm-comfort flags, where you should listen to your own body.
